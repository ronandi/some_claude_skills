# Changelog: skill-grader

## v1.0.0 (2026-02-05)

### Created
- 10-axis evaluation rubric with letter grades (A+ through F)
- Weighted overall grade computation (Description and Scope at 2x)
- Structured output format for full and abbreviated grading reports
- Grade scale with clear score-to-letter mapping
- Anti-patterns for grading itself (inflation, missing context, ignoring phantoms)
- Designed for sub-agent consumption: mechanical, repeatable, no domain expertise required
